Her Journey from playing Soccer to playing Video Games on her Youtube Channel

She was born on November 6, 2002 in Detriot, Michigan and grew up on Grosse Ile. She was raised in an upper-middle-class Christian family with her parents and two younger brothers, Tommy and Brandon. She played soccer as a forward for Grosse Ile High School, but a right-knee injury ended her sports career, ultimately leading her to explore gaming and social media. She graduated from Grosse Ile High School in 2020 and graduated Michigan State University in 2024.

Her TikTok journey began August 2019 when she posted her first video under the username annabananaxdddd as a joke. Her lip-sync, dance, and comedic videos quickly went viral, gaining her over 12.8 million followers and 840 million likes.
